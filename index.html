About Us

Welcome to EcoTrack, your reliable partner in tracking and managing water and electricity consumption. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to make informed decisions about their resource usage, promote sustainability, and contribute to a greener, more efficient future. Our Story At EcoTrack, we understand the significance of water and electricity in our daily lives. The idea for this platform was born out of a shared passion for environmental responsibility and the belief that small actions can make a big impact. Founded in 2024, we embarked on a journey to create a user-friendly and insightful platform that allows you to monitor, analyze, and optimize your water and electricity usage. Our team of dedicated professionals, including experts in environmental science, data analytics, and user experience design, came together to build a solution that brings simplicity to sustainable living.
